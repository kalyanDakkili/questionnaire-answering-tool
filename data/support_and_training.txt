CUSTOMER ONBOARDING
-------------------
Standard Onboarding (Starter/Professional):
- Self-service setup wizard
- Video tutorials library (50+ videos)
- Help center articles (docs.novamed.health)
- Email-based onboarding support for 30 days

Enterprise Onboarding:
- Dedicated Implementation Manager
- Customized training sessions (up to 8 hours)
- Data migration from legacy systems (supported formats: MedGenix, AdvancedMD, custom CSV)
- Go-live support (on-site or remote)
- 60-day hypercare period with weekly check-ins
