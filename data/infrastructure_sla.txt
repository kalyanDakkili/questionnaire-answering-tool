NOVAMED HEALTH - INFRASTRUCTURE & SLA DOCUMENTATION
====================================================

HOSTING & INFRASTRUCTURE
-------------------------
Cloud Provider: Amazon Web Services (AWS)
Primary Region: ap-south-1 (Mumbai, India)
DR Region: ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)
Database: AWS RDS PostgreSQL (Multi-AZ deployment)
Cache Layer: AWS ElastiCache (Redis)
Object Storage: AWS S3 with versioning enabled
CDN: AWS CloudFront for static assets

UPTIME SLA COMMITMENT BY PLAN
------------------------------
NovaMed Health guarantees the following uptime SLA commitment for enterprise and other customers:
Tier 1 - Starter Plan:       99.5% monthly uptime
Tier 2 - Professional Plan:  99.7% monthly uptime
Tier 3 - Enterprise Plan:    99.9% monthly uptime (approx. 8.7 hrs downtime/year)

SUPPORT RESPONSE TIME SLA BY SEVERITY LEVEL
--------------------------------------------
NovaMed Health provides the following support response time SLAs by severity level:
Severity 1 (Critical - system down):    1 hour response / 4 hour resolution (Enterprise)
Severity 2 (Major feature unavailable): 4 hours response / 24 hours resolution
Severity 3 (Minor issue):               8 hours response / 72 hours resolution
Severity 4 (General query):             24 hours response / 5 business days resolution

Support Channels:
- Enterprise: Dedicated CSM + phone support (24/7 for S1)
- Professional: Email + chat (business hours)
- Starter: Email only (business hours, Mon-Fri)

BACKUP & DISASTER RECOVERY STRATEGY
-------------------------------------
NovaMed Health backup and disaster recovery strategy includes:
- Automated daily database snapshots
- Point-In-Time Recovery (PITR): last 35 days
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour
- DR failover tested quarterly
- Secondary DR region: AWS Singapore (ap-southeast-1)

MAINTENANCE WINDOWS
-------------------
- Scheduled maintenance: Sundays 2 AM - 5 AM IST
- Emergency maintenance: 2-hour advance notice via email + status page
- Status page: status.novamed.health
