
NOVAMED HEALTH - COMPANY OVERVIEW
==================================
Industry: HealthTech / SaaS

NovaMed Health is a cloud-based Electronic Health Records (EHR) and practice management SaaS platform 
serving outpatient clinics, specialty practices, and telehealth providers across India and Southeast Asia.
Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Bengaluru, NovaMed serves over 1,200 active clinics with 
approximately 3,500 active healthcare providers on the platform.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
----------------------
1. NovaMed EHR - Core electronic health records system
2. NovaMed Telehealth - Integrated video consultation module
3. NovaMed Billing - Claims management and insurance billing
4. NovaMed Analytics - Practice performance dashboards
5. NovaMed Mobile - iOS and Android apps for providers and patients

BUSINESS MODEL
--------------
- Monthly SaaS subscription per provider seat: INR 2,500–8,000 depending on tier
- Implementation fee for onboarding: INR 15,000–50,000 one-time
- Custom integrations: priced on quote
- Annual enterprise contracts available with SLA guarantees

KEY METRICS (FY 2024)
-----------------------
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR): INR 18.2 Crore
- Monthly Active Users (providers): 3,500
- Average Net Promoter Score (NPS): 62
- Customer Churn Rate: 4.2% annually
- Average Contract Value (ACV): INR 1,52,000 per clinic per year
