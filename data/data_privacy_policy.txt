NOVAMED HEALTH - DATA PRIVACY & RETENTION POLICY
=================================================

DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS UNDER DPDP ACT 2023
-----------------------------------------
- Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data (fulfilled within 7 business days)
- Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data (fulfilled within 5 business days)
- Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data where legally permissible
- Right to Portability: Export personal data in machine-readable format (JSON/PDF)
- Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of non-essential processing at any time
- Right to Grievance Redressal: Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@novamed.health

DATA COLLECTION
---------------
NovaMed Health collects the following categories of data:
1. Patient Health Records (PHR): Medical history, prescriptions, lab results, visit notes
2. Provider Data: Doctor profiles, schedules, qualifications, billing information
3. Clinic Operational Data: Appointments, billing codes, insurance claims
4. Platform Usage Data: Feature usage, session logs, error logs (anonymized)
5. Communication Data: Appointment reminders, prescription notifications

DATA RETENTION
--------------
- Patient health records: Retained for 7 years post last patient interaction (per MCI guidelines)
- Billing and financial records: Retained for 8 years (per Indian tax law)
- Audit logs: Retained for 3 years
- Session logs: Retained for 90 days
- Deleted user data: Purged within 30 days of deletion request
- Backup data: Retained for 90 days with automated deletion

DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS
-------------------
Under DPDP Act 2023, patients and users have the following rights:
- Right to Access: Request a copy of their personal data (fulfilled within 7 business days)
- Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data (fulfilled within 5 business days)
- Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data where legally permissible
- Right to Portability: Export personal data in machine-readable format (JSON/PDF)
- Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of non-essential processing

DATA PROCESSING
---------------
- Data is processed only for stated purposes (healthcare delivery and billing)
- No data is sold to third parties under any circumstances
- Anonymized, aggregated data may be used for product improvement with explicit opt-in
- Third-party service providers are governed by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)
- Sub-processors are listed in our Third-Party Vendor Registry (available on request)

COOKIES AND TRACKING
--------------------
- Essential cookies only on patient-facing portal
- Analytics: Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled
- No advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels

DATA BREACH RESPONSE
--------------------
- Customers notified within 24 hours of confirmed breach affecting their data
- Regulatory notification (CERT-In) within 6 hours per IT Amendment Rules
- Post-incident report provided within 30 days
